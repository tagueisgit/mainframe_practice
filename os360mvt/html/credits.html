OS/360 on Hercules: Acknowledgments

13. Acknowledgments

I'd like to acknowledge the following folks who have contributed to the work so far on Hercules and OS/360:

  • Roger Bowler, Jan Jaeger, and the rest of the Hercules team, of course; without their work on Hercules, we wouldn't have anything to play with.
  • Rick Fochtman, for finding the OS/360 stuff and making the CD available.
  • Jack Schudel, for finding a copy of HASP.
  • Frank Pajerski, Sam Golob, and Sam Knutson, for keeping old copies of mods tapes around and available.
  • Kevin Leonard, who did the hard work of figuring out how to make the HASP 4.0 retrofit work. Greg Smith contributed debugging assistance. Gregg Berg found the cause of the persistent S200 ABEND, and Kevin squashed the bug once and for all.
  • Malcolm Beattie and Mike Ross, for getting TSO and TCAM built.
  • Tony Harminc and Glen Herrmannsfeldt, whose experienced advice on different parts of the process have helped immensely.
  • Dutch Owen, who discovered many of the problems with MVT so I didn't have to.
  • Alex Friis and Giorgio De Nunzio, for making the OS/360 CD available for me to grab before I got my copy.
  • Chris Craft, who found some early inconsistencies between what the docs should have said and what they actually did say, and spotted the control files I left out of the original copy of os360ctl.tar.gz.
  • Aron Eisenpress came up with a copy of the original SYS1.DFORTLIB.
  • The rest of the hercules-390 mailing list, for playing with the system and offering comments as they did.
